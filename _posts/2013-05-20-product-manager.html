We are a very rapidly growing start up company that's redefining how people buy apparel by building a scalable, highly personalized eCommerce platform. Up until now we have operated solely on our wits, our passion for building great products, and our desire to succeed as a team and a company. We are now at the point that hiring a couple of great product managers will be critical in helping the company get to the next level.

Responsibilities

  • Own the vision and the roadmap for a clearly defined area of the business
  • Work closely with co-workers to determine opportunities, prioritize, and roll-out products
  • Define metrics and success criteria for products and measuring/tracking to them
  • Ensure that critical details of new products or changes to existing products are communicated

We'd Like You to Have

  • 3-5 years experience managing a technology product
  • Outstanding written and verbal communication skills
  • the ability move effortlessly between thinking strategically and getting into the weeds
  • Drive to simplify at every opportunity - sometimes removing a feature is the best approach
  • Very effective in both Agile/story-based and ad-hoc environments
  • Able to recognize great design or it's absence and be able to articulate clearly design tradeoffs
  • Knows how to empower a very talented engineering team to build great products
  • Able to grasp and take into account the high-level technical implications of potential changes
  • Adept at digging into all kinds of data

Product-Centric Thinking

You come from a technology background but you have opinions about how the product you are building should work. You dig deep and work with customers and the company to uncover the ideal way to leverage technology to solve problems.

Self-Directed

Once you've got something, we don't have to worry about it. You have a bias toward action.

Passion

You love what you do. You spend some of your free time reading blogs/books, or going to meet ups.

Just Generally Awesome

You take your work seriously but not yourself. You show everyone respect regardless of the circumstances. Extra points if we think you're funny.

Location

While our preference is for everyone to work in San Francisco, we will make a exception if you are an exceptional fit.
