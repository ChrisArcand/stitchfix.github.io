We are a very rapidly growing start up company that's redefining how people buy apparel by building a scalable, highly personalized eCommerce platform. Up until now we have operated solely on our wits, our passion for building great products, and our desire to succeed as a team and a company.

You would be our first Product Manager and critical in helping us bring the company to the next level.

Responsibilities

  • Work directly with the CTO to define the vision and the roadmap for our suite of products
  • Work directly with stakeholders across the entire business to determine opportunities, prioritize, and roll-out products
  • Define metrics and success criteria for products and measure/track to them
  • Ensure that critical details of new products or changes to existing products are communicated

We'd Like You to Have

  • Outstanding written and verbal communication skills
  • The ability move effortlessly between thinking strategically and getting into the weeds
  • Drive to simplify at every opportunity - sometimes removing a feature is the best approach
  • Very effective in both Agile/story-based and ad-hoc environments
  • Able to recognize great design or it's absence and be able to articulate clearly design tradeoffs
  • Knows how to empower a very talented engineering team to build great products
  • Able to grasp and take into account the high-level technical implications of potential changes
  • Adept at digging into all kinds of data
