Engineering

Our multi-app, event-driven application architecture drives both scale and flexibility.

Multiple application architecture

There are no monoliths here. Each application in our platform has a clear and distinct purpose and all of them are built in house, primarily in Ruby. That means small, clean code bases with little technical debt and lots of flexibility. We use RabbitMQ to send messages about real-world events to other apps who decide for themselves whether or not to do something with it. This de-coupled architecture allows us to scale with ease and add or change components as needed.

We are the Product team

We hire engineers with great Product instinct because we are also the Product team. We value practicality and efficiency over process and interface directly with stakeholders in the business. Building great software is a collaborative effort. The business knows we need to do more than just deliver features and trusts where we focus our time and effort.

Deadlines are doomed to fail

Starting a conversation with a due date dooms it to fail from the beginning. We don't believe deadlines get things done faster. Rather, they encourage stress, bad decisions, and technical debt. We still deliver a lot in a short period of time, but not because we burned ourselves out in the process.

Culture

Bright, Kind, Goal-oriented.

Bright

We have really interesting problems to solve due to our unique business model and hire really bright people to tackle them. If it were easy everyone would be doing it, right?

Kind

It's not just smarts that makes you a good fit at Stitch Fix. We really value how well our team works together and kindness a huge part of it. Working with people we enjoy being around is a large part of our success.

Goal-oriented

We love people who are very driven and self-motivated. We are a largely flat organize and value autonomy combined with results. No one likes to be micro-managed and we don't want to start doing it!

TL;DR

We get stuff done. We have fun. We test. We learn. We work well together. We think big. We care about the product we are building. We push boundaries.
