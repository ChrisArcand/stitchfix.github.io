Stitch Fix is pioneering a new model of retail. We're a personal styling service with algorithms at the core of our business. As an Algorithm Developer at Stitch Fix you will play a central role and contribute to the recommendations that drive our service. We're looking for excellent people with a desire to get their hands dirty, build models and have a material impact on the business.

Your technical background

  • Experience with the entire modeling life-cycle: taking a model from concept to production and back again
  • Extensive experience with R, Python, Matlab, Julia or similar statistical tools: you are at home in your analysis environment and know what will be easy and what will be hard
  • Comfortable foraging for your own data: experience with SQL databases or other data platforms
  • Solid formal foundation: MS or PhD in statistics, math, computer science or other quantitative field

You are

  • Practical: recognize that simple is sometimes best (Occam was right)
  • Flexible: a scrappy developer who excels at projects spanning multiple teams and technologies
  • A great communicator: just as effective talking with a merchandise coordinator as with a software developer
