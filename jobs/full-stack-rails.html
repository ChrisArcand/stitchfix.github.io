Requirements

  • 5+ years software engineering experience
  • 2+ years Rails experience

You've been pivotal in building multiple products and have been around long enough to develop a Spidey Sense about most aspects of building complicated pieces of technology.

Communication Skills

You aren't just great at listening, speaking and writing. You are able to convey complex ideas and information regardless of the medium or situation

Product-Centric Thinking

You come from a technology background but you have opinions about how the product you are building should work. You dig deep and work with customers and the company to uncover the ideal way to leverage technology to solve problems.

Self-Directed

Once you've got something, we don't have to worry about it. You have a bias toward action.

Passion

You love what you do. You spend some of your free time reading blogs/books, or going to meet ups.

Just Generally Awesome

You take your work seriously but not yourself. You show everyone respect regardless of the circumstances. Extra points if we think you're funny.

Locations

San Francisco, Pittsburgh, or remote.
