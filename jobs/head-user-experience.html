The Role

This role would be responsible for Product Design across all technology products at Stitch Fix, including our customer facing products, our merchandising platform, our Styling platform used by a large remote workforce, and the suite of custom products that run our unique warehouse operations and shipping logistics. This position reports to the CTO.

Responsibilities

  • Manage and develop a small team of product designers. This requires setting clear goals, managing project loads and allocating designers accordingly, ensuring deadlines are met and setting up team members for overall success
  • Guide the team to develop best practices for ensuring a high bar of quality
  • Collaborate cross-functionally with all departments to ensure product designs are achieving the right goals
  • Partner closely with the marketing and customer service teams to ensure a consistent, high quality, brand-correct customer experience
  • Recruit new product designers and actively participate in the hiring process
  • Roll up your sleeves and contribute as a product designer
  • Execute in a fast-paced and highly fluid environment

Requirements

  • Strong portfolio that demonstrates systems thinking, analysis, and design across a range of interactive design formats (not just finished product screenshots)
  • Experience and expertise in high level product strategy
  • Familiarity with “Lean Design” processes—rapid prototyping, iterative development, and continuous product deployment—for digital products
  • Minimum 6 years industry experience in UX design
  • Minimum 2 years industry experience in management or leadership of a design team
  • Solid background in a relevant field, such as Design, HCI, Psychology, or commensurate work experience; degree a plus
  • Eloquent communication & presentation skills
  • Solid analytical skills regarding both quantitative and qualitative data
  • Effective time management skills to balance project schedule with fast-paced goals
