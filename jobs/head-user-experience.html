The Role

This role would be responsible for Product Design across all technology products at Stitch Fix, including our customer facing products, our merchandising platform, our Styling platform used by a large remote workforce, and the suite of custom products that run our unique warehouse operations and shipping logistics.

The person in this role will:

  • Define the vision and strategy for product design across multiple business units
  • Recruit an amazing team
  • Deliver a big upgrade to the entire customer experience
  • Champion the Stitch Fix design community
  • Be a strong advocate for Customer Experience and Design Thinking on the leadership team
  • Help everyone at Stitch Fix see Customer Experience as their responsibility
  • Help us redefine what a retail business can be
