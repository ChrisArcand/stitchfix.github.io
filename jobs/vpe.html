The Opportunity

Shopping for clothes is broken. Going to stores is time-consuming and often fruitless. Online shopping is mind-numbing, and it’s difficult to know if the style or size of an item is right. Underneath it all, we purchase clothes because we want to look good and feel good about how we look. In a time-starved world where no-one is helping us achieve this goal, there is a huge opportunity.

Stitch Fix is built from the ground up to solve this problem. Our service is deeply personal and permanently changes how people shop. We have unique, innovative platforms for remote styling, merchandising, warehouse and inventory management, and logistics. We leverage vast amounts of client data at key decision points throughout the company. All of this results in a simple, powerful offering to our clients and a very successful business, and we believe we are only scratching the surface of our opportunity!

The Team

We work closely with teams across the company. Our job is to solve business problems. We partner from strategy through delivery. Our most valuable asset is the trust of the people for whom we build products. We identify and deliver the highest value technology investments, and protect our ability to deliver that value over the long term. Some of our proudest moments come from solving business problems without writing a line of code.

We like to move quickly and ship lots of code, but in a considered way. We use techniques like automated testing and continuous deployment. We occasionally pair but not often. We believe in using whatever tools, processes, and products are pragmatic and get the job done. Some technologies you are likely to find here include Rails, iOS, PostgreSQL, Redis, ElasticSearch RabbitMQ, Heroku, GitHub, Blue Jeans, Screen Hero, and HipChat

The Role

As the VP of Engineering, Client Experience you are an amazing leader that will help us take the products and platforms that touch our clients to the next level. In addition to extending and improving from where we are, there are a handful of dramatic opportunities you will spearhead including:

  • Launch completely new products and services for our clients
  • Reinventing how we capture data from our clients
  • Introduce big upgrades to our one-of-a-kind Styling Platform

You are excited about this role because you will...

  • Lead and the engineering teams responsible for our websites, mobile apps, customer service tools, and styling platform.
  • Collaborate within Engineering and cross functionally to identify strategic goals, facilitate smart prioritization decisions, and build roadmaps
  • Ensure that investments are being made to enable long-term effectiveness of our products, people, and processes
  • Own an org design and hiring plan that will best tackle the opportunities at hand
  • Enable the organization to scale by developing leaders within the organization.
  • Take responsibility for hiring, retention, and performance management.
  • Help set the technical direction for the team and the evolution of the architecture
  • Create visibility of projects, progress and challenges, within and outside the engineering organization.
  • Manage the overall health of the software platform in a range of situations including normal functioning, customer escalations and crisis management.
  • Coach colleagues and business leaders on how to lead their teams, navigating and resolving employee relations issues or managing programs that help develop our people
  • Empower managers to build and foster great teams. You have seen the impact this has made on other organizations you’ve supported.

We are excited about you because you are...

  • You have at least 10 years experience as a product development leader
  • You have collaborated with business leaders to identify and achieve strategic business goals
  • You are passionate about inspiring and developing others to grow and become their best
  • You have high EQ and IQ, a strong collaborator and innovator
  • You have built, led, and scaled high-quality engineering teams from 15 to 40+
  • You have successfully built large-scale consumer products/services across multiple platforms (web, mobile, tablet)
  • You are passionate about recruiting world-class talent and have an aptitude for cultivating a culture of great recruiting
  • You have a strong product voice
  • You can fly high and be strategic or low and dive into the weeds; most importantly you know which is called for when
  • Cross-functional communication comes naturally to you in solving problems and addressing roadblocks
  • You’re highly self-motivated, have a stellar work ethic and you’re looking for the right company to support your growth
  • Above all else you are bright, kind and goal-oriented (our touchstone traits for everyone at Stitch Fix)
